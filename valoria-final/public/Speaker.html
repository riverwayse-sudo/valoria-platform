THE TIER SYSTEM

Not a directory.
A designation.

Every speaker on ATB Spotlight holds a tier designation — earned through track record, VALU Index score, and institutional review. Not purchased. Not self-assigned. The tier is what makes the platform useful to event organisers who need to know exactly what they are booking before the first conversation.
